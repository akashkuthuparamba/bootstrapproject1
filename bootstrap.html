WELCOME TO ALLEGRO MUSIC ACADEMY

Allegro Music Academy is a home for young, aspiring musicians and artists located in Tripunithura, a prominent historical and residential region in the city of Kochi, Kerala, India. Our goal is to provide quality instruction in music and other art education in a nurturing and supportive environment that encourages musical excellence in a non-competitive environment. We have experienced teachers who enable students to focus on mastering music and develop a feeling of belonging in a community through music sharing.
